Go East!

A one-stop leisure and lifestyle destination, Downtown East is also a choice venue for NTUC members and Singaporeans to enjoy a wide array of entertainment options at affordable prices.

From a humble beginning as just NTUC Pasir Ris Resort, it was re-developed in 2000 to become Downtown East, Singapore's lifestyle and recreational hub. Today, it is home to Costa Sands Resort and the popular water park, Wild Wild Wet. The entertainment and leisure centre E!hub opened in 2008 and added a cinema, Orchid Bowl and lots more to the colourful landscape.
