The coolest bowling centres for everybody

Orchid Bowl is one of the largest bowling operators in Singapore with five leisure bowling centres at The Chevrons, Safra Tampines, Safra Yishun, Orchid Country Club and E!hub, Downtown East.

It is also the first bowling operator in the region to be equipped with the latest bowling system, Switch - a high-tech system used in over 30 countries like Switzerland, Italy, Spain, Germany and the United Kingdom. All 140 lanes in Orchid Bowl's five centres boast lanes, furniture and equipment, designed by renowned car designer Pininfarina, who counts Ferrari and Maserati as his clients.

A centralised lane reservation system has also been installed, with the capability to allow bowlers to track their scores across all Orchid Bowl's centres and work out their bowling statistics over a chosen period.

Orchid Bowl @ Orchid Country Club

Located in Yishun, this 36-lane outlet is one of the largest bowling centres in Singapore, and the preferred choice for corporate events.

Orchid Bowl @ Safra Yishun Country Club

Surrounded by lush greenery, this is a place where families and friends can relax, dine and enjoy great bowling fun at this 24-lane outfit.

Orchid Bowl @ Safra Tampines

Right in the heart of a sporting hub, this outlet with 24 lanes is popular with sports enthusiasts, leisure fun-seekers and tertiary youths who are looking for a bowling good time in the east.

Orchid Bowl @ THE CHEVRONS

A vibrant and relaxing environment for the Chevron club members and the public to unwind and interact, this 24-lane outlet at Boon Lay sees mainly families and youngsters.

Orchid Bowl @ E!hub

Located at Downtown East and a trendy choice with both youths and families, this 32-lane bowling centre comes with the first-in-Singapore miniature bowling!
