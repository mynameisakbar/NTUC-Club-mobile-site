Located in the eastern part of Singapore, Costa Sands Resort (Downtown East) is a popular choice holiday destination for Singaporeans. It is the favourite resort getaway closer to home. Over the years, more facilities have been added to enhance the resort experience. Guests can now do a self check-in through the kiosks at the reception and breeze pass the queues at the check-in counter. Every room booked comes with a BBQ pit. There are also pro-family rooms available upon requests (subject to availability).

The elevated five-lane, 50-metre long swimming pool, a first in Singapore with see-through panels on its sides and bottom, allows resort guests to wave to their family members and friends when having fun swimming. Visitors are also spoilt for choice when it comes to food. The adjacent Downtown East is a gastronomic paradise with more than 40 outlets offering a kaleidoscope of food, ranging from food court dining to fast food joints to specialty restaurants. E!hub, a five-storey integrated lifestyle and entertainment centre located within th Downtown East landscape, comprises a cinema, a bowling centre and an indoor children playground - eXplorerkid. Our very own 25-metre tall indoor Ferris Wheel, another first of its kind in Singapore, is also housed in E!hub.

More action awaits visitors at the resort where there is a constant line-up of exciting events and activities held at the D'Marquee in Downtown East. Escape Theme Park, Singapore's first world-class theme park, is only a stone's throw away. It features 14 exhilarating rides and popular walk-through attraction, "Haunted II", which just underwent a facelift to provide more frightful fun for the brave ones.

Wild Wild Wet is the largest water theme park in Singapore. Ular-lah, the first raft slide in South East Asia, is the key attraction. Each raft seats six people and you can expect high-banked corners and 360 degree spins as you race to the finish line. Other thrilling rides include the Slide Up, a 11m high valley-shaped slide and the Waterworks, which consist of wacky curves and crazy turnings. For the less adventurous, enjoy comfortable rides in the Tsunami Wave Pool and Shiok River.

Costa Sands Resort (Downtown East) is also suitable for corporate and social retreats. To make every stay comfortable, the resort has 360 fully air-conditioned rooms with attached bathrooms, twin beds, televisions with DVD players, refrigerators and warm water flasks.

The resort offers a free two-way shuttle bus service to Pasir Ris MRT station.

Family-friendly amenities: The elevated swimming pool is accessible via a wheelchair-friendly elevator. The fitness corner within the Resort allows families to work out together. Temporary make-shift ramps for wheelchair-bound guests as well as wheelchairs and baby cots are available for loan upon guests' requests (subject to availability).

Perched on a hill overlooking Siloso Beach, Costa Sands Resort (Sentosa) is an ideal getaway from the city's hustle and bustle. The resort is also located close to Underwater World, the premier attraction in Sentosa. Amenities at the resort include a swimming pool, barbecue pits, a cafe, function rooms and video game machines.

In 2002, the resort underwent a $1 million renovation. There are currently 34 air-conditioned superior rooms complete with fully parquet floors as well as full or half-length windows.

For those who prefer a more traditional experience, there are 15 air-conditioned kampung huts which can house up to four persons comfortably.

Costa Sands Resort (Sentosa) is also suitable for corporate and social functions.

Nestled amidst the lush greenery of Pasir Ris Park and located near the beach, Costa Sands Resort (Pasir Ris) offers holiday makers and seekers an unforgettable experience with their families and friends. With its serene ambience, every stay is an enjoyable experience.

Facilities include a cafeteria, mini-mart, a convenient barbeque area outside each room, a swimming pool and children's pool, American pool tables, and launderette. The resort offers a free two-way shuttle service bus to Pasir Ris MRT Station. From the resort, it is a 5-minute walk to Escape Theme Park and Wild Wild Wet, both located in Downtown East.

Family-friendly amenities: There is a children pool available for infants and toddlers. The bicycle kiosk within the resort offers adult mountain bikes and children BMX bikes for rent. Inline skates of various sizes are also available for rent to both adults and children. To provide for more family-bonding activities, there is an extended area outside each room to facilitate group barbecuing. Wheelchairs and baby cots are available for loan upon guests' requests (subject to availability).
