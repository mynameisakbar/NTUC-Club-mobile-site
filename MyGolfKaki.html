Set up especially for union members, my golf kaki is a vibrant club established with the objective of building a sustainable golfing community with shared values and interests. As an associate member of the Singapore Golf Association (SGA), my golf kaki offers issuance and maintenance of handicap index. As part of our one-stop service, the club also offers golf insurance, preferential rates at local and overseas golf courses, merchant discounts and other exciting benefits for members.

Our perks at a glance


Maintenance of Handicap Index

Benefit from handicap maintenance for as low as $160 per annum!

Golf Insurance

Celebrate and spend up to $500 when you achieve a Hole-in-One at any golf course worldwide!

Concessionary Green Fees

Enjoy attractive green fees from as low as $36 at Orchid Country Club. Other concessions are also available at selected local and foreign golf courses.

Activities and Programmes

Look out for the monthly medals and members' nights.

Special Rates for Proficiency Certification

Obtain Proficiency Certification with up to 50% savings with appointed golf academies.

Retail Discounts

Shop at MST Golf and get an instant 25% discount on purchase of golfing merchandise.
