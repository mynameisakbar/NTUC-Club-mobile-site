Set up especially for union members, my golf kaki is a vibrant club established with the objective of building a sustainable golfing community with shared values and interests. As an associate member of the Singapore Golf Association (SGA), my golf kaki offers issuance and maintenance of handicap index. As part of our one-stop service, the club also offers golf insurance, preferential rates at local and overseas golf courses, merchant discounts and other exciting benefits for members.

Our perks at a glance
