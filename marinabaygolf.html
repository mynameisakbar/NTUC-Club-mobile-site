More than just a good game of golf

Marina Bay Golf Course is Singapore's only 18-hole public golf course and one of Asia's best public golf courses. Located strategically in the heart of town, the course offers golfers great accessibility and a picturesque view of the city skyline, be it day or night.

Being the only links-styled golf course in Singapore, the design of the par 72 course attempts to connect to the history of golf - traditional links of Scotland and the beginning of golf - with its few water hazards and in particular, its 91 pot bunkers. These bunkers not only serve as one of our dramatic features, they are also geared to provide hours of challenges and excitement to golfers.

Besides being one of the few golf courses in Singapore to offer night golfing from Wednesday to Friday for busy executives, Marina Bay Golf Course also offers golfers an enhanced golfing experience through the use of the Global Positioning System on its full fleet of buggies.

The four-tier driving range with 146 bays is the biggest and the longest driving range in Singapore. The driving range is open from 7am to 10.45pm all days, except on Monday mornings. The course also offers four practice greens for golfers.

Key facilities & services


Practice Facilities

Amateur golfers can practice at putting greens, chipping green and practice bunker. Charges may apply for the use of these facilities.

The Canopy Cafe

Relax and enjoy a light snack while enjoying a bird's eye view of the golf course. The cafe serves a variety of local and western food so you'd be spoilt for choices.

MST Golf

This retail store offers a myriad of golfing products, from equipment to accessories and apparel. Rental of clubs and shoes is also available.

StarGolf Academy

The academy offers various training programmes to suit all levels as well as customised training modules for golfers with specific goals. Lessons are conducted by experienced golf professionals who are recognised by the Singapore Professional Golfers' Association (SPGA).

Canopy Cafe

Golfers can grab a bite to eat before or after their game at The Canopy Cafe. Situated on the second floor of the Canopy, this is the spot to kick back, relax and take in the spectacular sights of the golf course. You'll also be spoilt for choice with a wide spread of local and western food. Try their signature dishes like Johor Bee Hoon and Penang Kway Teow!

MST Golf

This little slice of golfing heaven retails a wide selection of golfing products ranging from equipment, to accessories and apparel. If you're in the mood for renting instead, you can also have your pick of clubs and shoes here.

Star Golf Academy

Various training programmes with customised modules are offered to help golfers of all age groups to achieve their specific goals. Such lessons are conducted by a dedicated team of highly qualified and experienced golf professionals recognised by the Professional Golfers' Association (PGA).
