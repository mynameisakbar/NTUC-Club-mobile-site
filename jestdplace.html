Where music is just a decibel away

Oriental meets Zen when it comes to Jest D'Place - a Mando pop haven that features Chinese calligraphy. The clubhouse, spanning more than 8,000 sq feet, redefines entertainment through its unique offerings, with drinks prices still kept affordable for members. Resident artistes perform a mix of Mandarin & Hokkien evergreens and the latest pop songs on Canto Nite, Hokkien Nite and Social Nite.

The early part of the night sees the social dance community vibrant with fellow music lovers. Engagement on the dance floor is not just fun, but helps promote camaraderie among the union members along with one another's friends and families.

With occasional live superstar acts, Jest D'Place is the place to be seen and heard. Fridays are reserved for Dangdut aficionados. The weekly event Party Sensasi features a repertoire of music (Rock Melayu, Lagu Pop, Klasik & R&B) staged by our in-house DJ and resident Malay band Kalahari.
