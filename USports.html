Bringing together a community of
sports enthusiast

U Sports was set up to engage sports enthusiasts and their families and friends. It provides a comprehensive and packed calendar of sports-related events and activities. Those who have a common passion for sports can come together and actively nurture new friendships. Initiatives such as the various sports interest groups and mass events, leagues and competitions such as the U Games, the iconic inter-union, multi-sport tournament are also in place to encourage sustained participation among members. U Sports aims to increase outreach and create opportunities for union members to enhance interaction and forge a community built on strong ties of friendship and a fervour for sports.

We organise free trials, clinics, workshops, camps and competitions for the following interest groups on a regular basis. Indeed, an exciting calendar of events awaits the interest of group members.

U Games


Organised by U Sports, U Games is the iconic multi-sports competition in the Labour Movement calendar. The main goals of these games are to promote sportsmanship among the affiliate unions and foster camaraderie among the members. There is an exciting range of sports in the lineup, with about 13 sporting events (see below) in the U Games calendar. Unions accumulate points through these competitions culminating in one union being named the overall Champion at the end of the year. Other major events include 3-on-3 Basketball and Golf.

Iconic Events

Since 2009, U Sports has launched successful initiatives that continue to grow in scale and popularity. The annual U Run features a refreshing and challenging category comprising a 10km route followed by a vertical race up the stairs. 2011 saw the Indoor Fiesta engaging participants of all ages in a full day of fitness workout with activities ranging from Hatha Yoga to Body Combat.

Through the Sports Challenge (Youth Sports Conference), sports enthusiasts underwent fun challenges and learnt how to maximise their sporting potential. Workshops such as Sports Psychology, Strength and Conditioning, Sports Rehabilitation, Sports Marketing help to enhance the sporting achievements of our young participants.
