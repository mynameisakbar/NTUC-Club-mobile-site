Salsa at Union Square

Even non dancers can't stop their feet tapping and hips swaying to the hypnotic and pulsating rhythms of salsa and merengue every night from 9pm onwards at Singapore's premium home of Latin beats. The audience get to enjoy Mando Pop Acoustic during Happy Hours from 6pm to 9pm on weekdays.

Opened in 2000 and voted Best Salsa Club in 2008, Union Square at The Amara is the only place in Singapore where you can get salsa music six days a week provided by DJs and live bands from Latin America. Mando Pop Acoustic, Latin Bands from Columbia and Live DJ all happen under one roof at Union Square! Whether for a date, fun with friends, or an opportunity to meet Latin dance fans, Union Square will have you coming back for more.
